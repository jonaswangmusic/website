About Me

Jonas Wang (Junyi Wang) is a year 4 undergraduate student majoring Music Technology at Royal Birmingham Conservatoire. Besides his study, Jonas Wang is making music that drew impact from several styles and genres, namely Electronic, Pop, Jazz, Funk, Classical, and Oriental.

As an independent artist, Jonas Wang had released music on different labels and platforms including NCS, Zero Cool, Metanoia, and many more.
